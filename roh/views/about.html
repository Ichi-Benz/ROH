About Us

Interesting Image

Region of Hoops is a faction of individuals who seek to bring together young and older people in the community of Brampton and the rest of the GTA to a level of inspiration where they feel empowered because of their involvement in the community.

We as a company want to create an exciting experience for basketball lovers world-wide and convey the players on a type of pedestal that they have never been put on before.
